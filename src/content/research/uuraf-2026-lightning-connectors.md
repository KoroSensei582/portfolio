Presented a poster at MSU's University Undergraduate Research and Arts Forum comparing the microstructure and elemental composition of authentic versus third-party Apple Lightning connector probes.

The analysis used scanning electron microscopy (SEM) for surface morphology and energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy (EDS) for elemental mapping of contact plating.
